A Sari,Saree,Sharee ia a women's garment from the Indian subcontinent that consists of an unstitched drape varying from 4.5 to 9 meters in length 1,200 millimeters in breadth that is typically wrapped around the waist,with one end draped over the shoulder,partly baring the midriff.There are various styles of sari manufature and draping,the most common being nivi style,which originated in the Deccan region.
